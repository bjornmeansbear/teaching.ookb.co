Since a young age I have always been interested in the world of art. My older sister would draw pictures or practice writing the alphabet before I was old enough to go to school. This had interested me and I would practice drawing her pictures and letters.

As time went on the alphabet no longer became letters but was an art form in itself. In high school I had won many awards for the work I had done and currently to this day many pieces still hang up in New York schools, but something was missing. After a short while I found myself in the military as a Army Sniper Team Leader. There was a soldier who was very artistic and one again I was unable to escape this dying need to produce art. While in Iraq this soldier and I had produced many works of art and helped to build a church, painting such things as stained glass and the last judgment outside of the church. After 5 years I had decided to take that drive and get out of the military to chance my one true passion, Art!

Since times have changed, so have I, although there are many fields in art I find myself more and more interested in the design world. Constantly reading and seeing what's out there. To be competent you need to seek self-improvement and as a graphic design I strive to do that every day. I am currently a student at ESU for art and design and will graduate in 2013, which I will than move on to a graduate program for graphic design.
