Overview

The show focuses on the life of a group of young adults through the eyes of a typical gay guy. His father Walter, a lawyer has married Emma, a beautiful caring woman. The series was created by Michael Bay, along with the writing team of Sara V. Finney and Vida Spears. The settings for the show include the MICA campus as well as places like Grand Central and terrible MICA parties. The sitcom was portrayed to take place in the Leimert Park area of Los Angeles, California.

The show dealt with real young adult social issues such as sex, homosexuality, race relations, friendship, tragedy, and day-to-day issues college kids. In one of the most controversial episodes, "Secrets and Lies," the Reynolds family learned from Aunt Cora that Lamar is the biological father of Julius , whom the Reynolds and Dorian himself believed to be Lamar's nephew. The shocking news of Julius's infidelity during his first marriage turned the family upside-down and resulted in Julius's rebellion and Alexander's relocation from his home.
