Drohan J. DiSanto

I am a graphic designer, tattooer, musician, and history nerd living in Baltimore, Maryland. Before studying Graphic Design at Maryland Institute College of Art, I earned my BA in Critical & Visual Studies at Pratt Institute in Brooklyn, NY, cut my teeth at Big Joe & Sons Tattoo in White Plains, NY, and worked for a non-profit organization focusing on bringing reigonal produce and ecological awareness to New York City as well as education and urban farming.
