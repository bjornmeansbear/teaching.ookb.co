I am a Graphic Designer and Web Coder from Pennsylvania. Currently I hold a BA form East Stroudsburg University and I am currently enrolled in the Graphic Design MFA program at Maryland Institute College of Art (MICA). Prior to starting my career in design, I spent 5 years in the US Army.

Since entering into this exciting and innovative field, I have developed a strategy that is based around identifying a problem and solving it. Since breaking into this field I have collaborated and worked with many different clients.

My site is still under construction. But if are more than welcome to visit my work by going to my Behance or Tumblr Blog. If you have any questions feel free to email me at hbecker@mica.edu with questions about new projects. Web site coming soon.
