Hi. I'm Rheagen King. A Kansas native, I was born in the small town suburbs of Derby. I originally intended to become a photographer, specializing in editorial and fashion photo, but I accidentally stumbled across design in high school.
I began competing in numerous design-journalism competitions and was accepted by the Maryland Institute College of Art in 2015 where I currently study Graphic Design. My work has placed in several local, regional and national competitions, such as Scholastics, JEA/NSPA, KSPA and MICA's Competitive Scholarship.
I create work that serves a dual purpose, conveying emotion and pushing social boundaries. I intend to continue pushing those boundaries and expanding my expertise by serving innovators where I can help them establish new ideas and set trends.
