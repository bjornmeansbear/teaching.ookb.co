Sneakers, which were originally designed for athletes and trainers, have became the must-have items over the past decade. Suits, jackets and even ballgowns, those trendsetters could mix almost every look with those practical and comfortable shoes.

Finally, the hit of sneakers officially showed up on runway. From Chanel to Christian Dior, they made sport shoes that no one would work out with.

When it comes to my sneakers, black and white are all I need. Simple, full of constrast, classic and timeless, just like what I think of myself.
