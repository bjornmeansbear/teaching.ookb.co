Alice's Adventures is an experiment in digital storybook interaction, using HTML, CSS3, and jQuery. jQuery plugins include Mark Dalgeish's stellar.js, Jack Rugile's jRumble, GSGD's jQuery Easing, and Malsup's jQuery Cycle. Special thanks to Kristian Bjornard.
Jackie Littman is a Baltimore-based graphic designer and illustrator. She completed this project in the MFA Graphic Design program at the Maryland Institute College of Art.
